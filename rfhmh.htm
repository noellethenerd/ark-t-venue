The Venue has four rooms and a main hall that can be booked separately or together.
Please note: rooms 1 and 2 must both be available during the times that you want to book the main hall.

Main Hall

This is a large, light, and modern space overlooking the gardens. This room is perfect for a whole range of activities and events. It's a perfect meeting place for clubs and societies or as a venue for private parties. It's equipped with air conditioning and, for a small extra charge, you get full use of the stylish kitchen space – complete with a serving hatch, making it ultra-easy to serve refreshments!
